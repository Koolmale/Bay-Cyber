Who We Are

Established in 2016, our region is comprised the San Francisco and Monterey Bay areas interconnected by our common passion for Cybersecurity. Our Mission is to at least make our students, and by extension our communities, safer than when they joined. At best, we guide students towards a career in Information Technology and Cybersecurity. Our students, staff and faculty are in Middle School, High School and Community College. We have a growing connection with our communities, industry and beyond who are essential to ensuring long-term support for the program.

Cyber Competitions

Competitions challenge our students to critically think in a team environment while applying their classroom knowledge. We participate in CSAW, PicoCTF, CyberStart, and CyberPatriot.
